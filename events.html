Events

Night in the museum

Join us for a scientific adventure in the museum, where we'll have fun and learn. At night.

Explore science with us.

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays.

Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

The Metaverse

Join us and our guest speaker as we discuss the Metaverse. Will all of us soon be living in a virtual world?

Will all of us soon be living in a virtual world?

Join us for an exciting talk about the possibility of humanity choosing a virtual reality as its final destination.

As advanced as VR technology is today - how far away are we from considering living inside a virtual and digital space? Professor Patrick Danielson hopes to take you on a journey through what might be our future.

Lunar Eclipse

Come and share the experience of observing the lunar eclipse with us. At night.

Observe the moon in Earth’s shadow.

In May 2022 we’ll be fortunate enough to experience a partial lunar eclipse in Oslo and we want to share that experience with you.

Bring your friends and family for a night you’ll never forget, as we look to the night sky in awe of our beautiful moon in different states. Expect to be up late.

Dinosaur Tour

Join our paleontologist on a spectacular guided tour and experience dinosaurs like never before - with an expert!

Get to know our oldest predecessors.

Our paleontologist Ty Rex will be taking you on a journey back to pre-historic earth, when Dinosaurs were roaming the earth.

Be prepared to be blown away by the wonders of well-preserved fossils. In addition to a guided tour through our Evolution-exhibit, you’ll be able to observe exclusive fossils that haven’t been displayed publicly before.

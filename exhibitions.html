Exhibitions

Cosmology

Do you want to learn more about outer space and the great cosmos?

Explore the wonders of our cosmos.

Our fantastic exhibition ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Follow the journey of our solar exploration - from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Robotics and AI

Come and join us for a sneak peek into the future - and meet Rob the Robot!

Explore the future of technology.

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here.

Follow the journey of our solar exploration - from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Evolution

Visit our largest exhibition yet to get a glimpse of pre-historical earth.

How about a sneak peek into the past?

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time.

You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn’t survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

Biology and medicine

Discover more about biology and how we combat disease.

Dive into the world of biology.

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world.

Over a series of exhibits we explore the history of medicine and take a look at some major breakthroughs including the discovery of penicillin and the first heart transplant.

Many of our great medicinal discoveries have come not only from the lab, but also from observing animals in the wild. Often our fellow creatures have beat us to it.
